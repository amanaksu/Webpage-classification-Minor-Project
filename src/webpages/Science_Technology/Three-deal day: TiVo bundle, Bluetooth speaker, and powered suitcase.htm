Three-deal day: TiVo bundle, Bluetooth speaker, and powered suitcase

Powered suitcase? Yep, it's a rolling travel bag with a built-in battery for keeping your mobile devices charged.

by Rick Broida
October 9, 2013 6:36 AM PDT
  • Follow @cheapskateblog

What's better than two-deal Tuesday? Three-deal Wednesday, of course! Let's get right to it, because at least one of them is likely to sell out soon.

TiVo bundle

The TiVo Stream is a great accessory, and it's included with this bundle.

(Credit: Woot)

If you've been lusting after one of those higher-end new TiVo Roamio DVRs with the built-in TiVo Stream capabilities, here's your chance to get the poor-man's equivalent.

While supplies last, Tech.Woot has the TiVo Premiere 4 and TiVo Stream for $99.99, plus $5 for shipping. Alternately, on the same page, you can opt for a refurbished but much higher-capacity TiVo Premiere XL4 and TiVo Stream for $199.99 plus shipping.

The TiVo Stream has a list price of $129.99 all by itself. Read CNET's review if you want to learn more.

You're still on the hook for TiVo's ridiculously overpriced service (see the details in Woot's description area), but at least here you're getting the gear on the cheap.

Bluetooth speaker

(Credit: Tanga)

A few months back I shared a Tanga deal for a dirt-cheap portable Bluetooth speaker.

It's ba-ack! For a limited time, and while supplies last, Tanga once again has the Urge Basics Soundbrick portable Bluetooth speaker for $33.99, plus $2.99 for shipping.

Similar in size and design to the Jawbone Jambox, the Soundtrick comes in your choice of four colors. It runs on a rechargeable battery, doubles as a speakerphone, and includes both line-in and line-out jacks.

If you grabbed one of these last time around, hit the comments to let your fellow cheeps know how it worked out -- and whether they should jump at it this time.

Powerbag rolling office case

(Credit: Woot)

I love Powerbag bags, which embed big rechargeable batteries and device-charging tips into quality luggage.

If you're already at Tech.Woot for the TiVo, you can also grab the Powerbag rolling office case for $39.99, plus $5 for shipping. Price at Amazon: $79.

Built for business travelers, this rolling suitcase includes a 6,000mAh rechargeable battery and built-in tips for powering miniUSB, microUSB, and Apple 30-pin devices. There's also a standard USB port for powering everything else.

I'm grabbing one of these for those times when I prefer a wheeled alternative to my Powerbag backpack. Your thoughts?

Bonus deal: Yes, even on three-deal Wednesday, a bonus! Ice Monkey has the refurbished Logitech Harmony 650 universal remote for $39.99 shipped. That's one of the better deals I've seen on a Harmony, especially one with a color screen. List price: $79.99.
