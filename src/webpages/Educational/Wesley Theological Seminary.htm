    At Wesley, classroom and field learning become one in preparing students for the real practice of ministry. Wesley’s Washington, D.C. location provides the seminary with a presence in the public square, where we study the intersection of theology and politics. Wesley offers four degree programs – the Master of Divinity, Master of Theological Studies, Master of Arts and Doctor of Ministry.
  • Academics
    At Wesley, our degree programs are tailored to fit a variety of vocational goals. The range of educational programs gives voice to our understanding that all people are called to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ to a broken world – in their own way. A Wesley education provides the foundation necessary to commit to your calling, whatever it may be.

    Wesley graduates are in ministry in all 50 states and in 20 countries as leaders of churches and service organizations.
