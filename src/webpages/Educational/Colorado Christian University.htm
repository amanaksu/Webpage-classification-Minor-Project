  • CCU Quick Facts

    • A private university with more than 5,000 traditional, adult and graduate students
    • CCU traces our heritage back to 1914 when Denver Bible Institute was founded
    • Main campus only 10 minutes from the Rocky Mountains and downtown Denver
    • Offers associate, bachelor's, and master's degrees and licensure programs
    • A member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities
    • One of the only Christian colleges in an eight-state region
    • Adult and Graduate Studies centers throughout Colorado
    • Participates in NCAA Division II and NCCAA Division I Athletics
