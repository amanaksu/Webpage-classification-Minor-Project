FROM THE PRESIDENT'S OFFICE

Thank you for visiting the Hobe Sound Bible College website! As you move through our site, we hope you will find the information you need, but we hope you will find more than just information. We hope you will capture something of the warmth, the friendly atmosphere, and the beautiful surroundings that make Hobe Sound Bible College such a wonderful place. Gimcheon University Student Exchange

Hobe Sound Bible College and alumnus Professor Robert Black of Gimcheon University recently hosted President Kang, Professor Lee, Professor Park and Professor David Lee of Gimcheon University. This visit was the final step in the process of creating a relationship between Gimcheon University, located in South Korea, and Hobe Sound Bible College. The nature of this relationship will be student exchange. Students who are interested in completing a TESOL practicum from Hobe Sound Bible College will be hosted at the Gimcheon University campus and students from Gimcheon University will be spending one semester in Hobe Sound where they will be awarded a certificate of completion in the area of TESOL studies. Gimcheon University's theme is "Changing the World Through Love" and it is God's love they want to show the world. This is a shared vision with Hobe Sound Bible College and it is in keeping with the Hobe Sound Bible College theme of "Knowing Christ and Making Him Known."
