American Archives Month 

For the month of October, the Benedictine University Library will be celebrating American Archives Month. Students, faculty and staff will be able to participate in a variety of contests including best photo caption and BenU trivia. The month long event will end wrap up on Wednesday, October 30 with an open house. Tours of the archives, including a behind-the-scenes look at how items are collected and preserved, will be offered. The open house is open to the public.
