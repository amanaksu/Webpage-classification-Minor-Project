Nursing and Health Professions Degrees

Educating for Success. Nebraska Methodist College is one of the top 25 nursing and allied healthcare colleges in the nation.  Whether you're pursuing a Nursing degree or a career in  Allied Health or other Health Professions, NMC is the healthcare college for you. That's because healthcare education is our only focus. We help you realize your educational goals through a supportive student environment, expert faculty committed to your success, and a cutting-edge education, on campus and online.
