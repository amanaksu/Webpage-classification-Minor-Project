Living Labor History Residency

National Labor College students from numerous unions across the country spent Veterans Day weekend learning from their classmates at the Living Labor History class held at Maritime Institute of Technology and Graduate Studies. The four-day course is part of the requirements for students pursuing degrees in the School of Labor Studies.

NLC is now accredited to offer an Associate of Arts degree! Semester begins September 3, 2013.

National Labor College (NLC) is now accredited to offer an Associate of Arts degree. An Associate of Arts degree is basically the first two years of college and will provide students with the foundation to succeed in NLC's bachelor degree programs or at any other 4-year college or university.

Applications for the Associate of Arts degree for the fall semester beginning September 3, 2013 are now available!

In order to apply, union members, their families and others must have a high school diploma or GED certificate.

Discover more about the Associate of Arts degree and apply today!
