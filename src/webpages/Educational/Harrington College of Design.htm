We can give you the whole picture.

Harrington College of Design can immerse you in the complete professional process. Design is just part of it. We offer a holistic educational experience where you can learn both the business and creative sides of your profession. The basics of aesthetics, strategic thinking and concrete technical skills are taught by instructors who know what it takes. Our undergraduate and graduate programs are designed to help you move forward in the fields of Communication Design, Graphic Design, Interior Design, Photography, or Web Design and Development.

We'll help you figure out the details.

At Harrington College of Design, we know our students don’t want anything to get in the way of their success. That's why we have experienced financial aid representatives to help you figure out the best way to learn about the possible options for paying for your education, including scholarship opportunities, grants, loans, tuition reimbursement or federal work-study programs. Financial aid is available for those who qualify.

Learn more about Financial Aid.

Show your professional potential.

At Harrington College of Design, you can find the support you need, so you can connect with the industry you love. Our Career Services staff can help students and graduates with preparing for their career search, from putting together a sharp portfolio to marketing their unique talents.

Learn more about Harrington’s Career Services.

Get your questions answered.

At Harrington College of Design, we seek students with an aptitude for creativity and a commitment to achieving professional and personal goals. Talk to our experienced admissions representatives for more details about our undergraduate and graduate programs in Communication Design, Graphic Design, Interior Design, Photography, or Web Design and Development.
