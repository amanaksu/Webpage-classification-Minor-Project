Northwood University strives to incorporate the lessons of the American free-enterprise society in the college classroom to develop future leaders, managers, and entrepreneurs. Northwood has residential college campuses in Texas, Florida, and Michigan.

Northwood also offers a wide range of adult degree programs to fit your needs, including the nation's only Automotive Marketing and Management program and North America's first and only Franchising Management Bachelor's of Business Administration degree program.

Through the DeVos Graduate School, students have the opportunity to earn their graduate business degree in traditional, day-time classes through the accelerated MBA;evening MBA classes; weekend MBA program; or in our online Master of Science in Organizational Leadership program. If you are looking for a business school in Texas, Florida, or Michigan,contact us today!
