This website has been created specifically for our students, faculty and staff members. Here you can find information about student services, student housing and career services available at DeVry Addison, including assistance with part-time employment and online access to your student account. The site also provides access to extensive library resources and to the bookstore where you can purchase textbooks online as well as services offered at our Centers in Downers Grove, Elgin, Naperville and Schaumburg.

If you're looking for detailed DeVry University information on program offerings as well as admissions and financial information, please visit our locations on the DeVry.edu site:
Addison Campus, Downers Grove Center, Elgin Center, Naperville Center and Schaumburg Center.

If you're looking for detailed Keller Graduate School of Management information on program offerings as well as admissions and financial information, please visit our locations on Keller.edu:
Downers Grove Center, Elgin Center, Naperville Center and Schaumburg Center.

For the latest school emergency, school closing or inclement weather notification messages, please check this DeVry website.
