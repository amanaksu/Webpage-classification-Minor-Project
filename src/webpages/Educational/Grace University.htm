Welcome to Grace University

Grace University is a premier Midwest Christian college located in Omaha, Nebraska. Our goal is to develop servant leaders for the home, the church and the world through excellence in biblically-integrated education. Here students receive a life changing experience in a personal, discipling environment, all for the glory of God.
