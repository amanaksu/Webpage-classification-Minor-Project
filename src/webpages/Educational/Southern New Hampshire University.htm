Supporting Our Troops

Current active-duty military students impacted by the loss of tuition assistance due to the government shutdown will receive a scholarship equal to tuition for one course. This means that Southern New Hampshire University will cover the cost for one course.

 

 

 

 

Our one focus is you.

With more than 100 undergraduate programs, a supportive community, and a vibrant campus experience, SNHU focuses on one thing – giving you every opportunity to succeed.

Get the credit you deserve.

Isn’t it time your education started working for you? Transfer up to 90 credits into one of our 100-plus undergraduate programs. Attend online, on campus or at one of our centers.

A U.S. university; a global experience.

Our students and faculty come from all over the world, representing more than 60 countries. We're an award-winning university, with affordable degree programs that can help you go places.
