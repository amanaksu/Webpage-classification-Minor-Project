Direction for Life

For more than 100 years, The College of St. Scholastica has been guided by its Catholic Benedictine roots.

A welcoming community of more than 4,000 learners, CSS is a place to discover yourself and your place in the world. With everything from traditional on-campus settings to programs designed for working professionals, you'll find a degree path that fits your needs from a college recognized as one of the finest in the Midwest.
