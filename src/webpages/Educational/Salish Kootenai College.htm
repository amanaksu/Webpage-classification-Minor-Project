Salish Kootenai College offers a variety of academic programs to help you reach your educational goals. We offer students 5 vocational programs, 18 Associate Degrees, and 14 different Baccalaureate Degrees. Each of our academic programs have faculty that are dedicated to our students success, as well as the Mission of Salish Kootenai College. We are currently the only Tribal College to offer Bachelor Degrees in; Hydrology, Forestry, Nursing, Life Science, as well as Psychology. In addition to those programs, Salish Kootenai College is the first and only educational institution to offer an Associate & Bachelor Degree in the emerging field of Tribal Historic Preservation. Please look through the specific degree programs for more detailed information on any program that may interest you.
