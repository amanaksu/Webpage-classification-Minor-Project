  • Homecoming & Family Weekend

    All alumni, families, friends and fans are invited to Homecoming & Family Weekend, October 10-13.

    Events are planned all week long, with the celebration kicking into high gear on Friday with Festival on the Oval, the "#StalwartRam" parade, bonfire, lighting of the 'A' and Rams vs Cowgirls volleyball match.

    More About Homecoming & Family Weekend

  • Cans Around the Oval

    Created and run by students, Cans Around the Oval is a partnership with the Food Bank for Larimer County, collecting food and monetary donations to help our community. Last year the drive collected 52,188 pounds of food and $41,806 in donations, helping more than 40,000 people who depend on the food bank.

    More About Cans Around the Oval

  • Best Teacher Brian Tracy

    Brian Tracy, an associate professor in the Department of Health and Exercise Science, does more than teach just the facts of human movement and anatomical kinesiology, he is focused on helping students develop critical thinking skills related to the field.

    More About Dr. Tracy

  • Among the Nation's Best

    CSU ranks among the nation's best in the 2013 U.S. News and World Report annual America's Best Colleges rankings. The latest rankings place CSU as 60th among public universities nationwide and list the university as an A-plus school for students who want to be a part of a university with a broad, engaged student body.

    Learn more

  • Colorado International Invitational Poster Exhibition

    The 18th biennial Colorado International Invitational Poster Exhibition hosted by the Department of Art at Colorado State University features works of top poster artists and designers worldwide.

    More About CIIPE

News

Homecoming Friday - Festival, Parade and Bonfire

The CSU community will show what it means to be a stalwart Ram at Friday's Homecoming and Family Weekend festival, parade, pep rally and bonfire. Students, employees, alumni and community members of all ages are invited to join in on the fun.

Homecoming Time Capsule

Homecoming editions of the Rocky Mountain Collegian, the student-run newspaper founded in 1891, disclose a wealth of historical perspective on what has transpired at Colorado State since the University's first building was erected on campus in 1874.

Homecoming Saturday - Come Early, Stay Late!

Beginning with the annual Homecoming 5K Race at 8 a.m. and ending with the Rams volleyball game at 7:30 p.m., Saturday has something for everyone - the campus and community are invited to run, walk, tailgate and cheer the day away.

Parade Street Closures and Parking Information

Several roads will be temporarily closed in Fort Collins during the Homecoming and Family Weekend parade on Friday. Alternative modes of transportation, such as riding the Transfort bus or bicycling, are recommended throughout the day.
