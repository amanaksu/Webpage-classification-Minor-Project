University of the District of Columbia

An urban land-grant university, offering associate, 4-year bachelor's, master's, professional and law degrees through its academic colleges including the Community College and David A. Clarke School of Law.

New Student Center

The University is excited about it's New Student Center and the transformation it will bring to the campus.  The first phase of construction has already begun.  For more information regarding this project, visit our Student Center web page.

Brick & Paver Campaign

Celebrate your connection with the University or commemorate a loved one by purchasing a brick/paver that will be placed on the renovated Cleveland L. Dennard Plaza.  Go to www.udc.edu/bricks and reserve your brick or paver today!

Faces of UDC

James D. Gaston, III '11

James D. Gaston, III (M.P.A. ’11) is the 2011-2012 Morehouse College Alumni Association DC Chapter (MCAADC) Man of the Year.  Gaston serves as the Alumni Association's (UDCNAS) Fund-Raising Co-Chair.  Be sure to read the rest of this new alum's story.

Mindy Hill '10

Mindy Hill ’10, embarked on a month-long trip to Jakarta, Indonesia, for the “Exchange of a Lifetime” project and cultural fellowship opportunity.  Read more on Hill’s story and her career objective.

 

Dr. Cristi Ford

Dr. Cristi Ford, Assistant Professor in the Center for Academic Technology (CAT) at the University of the District of Columbia Learning Resources Division was recently highlighted in the November Sloan-C newsletter.  Read more about Dr. Ford being recognized in the November Sloan-C Letter.

Lt. Donald Whiting

Lt. Donald Whiting has been a part of UDC for 30 years and has been in Law Enforcement most of his adult life. He is a member of Public Safety team and currently manages the day work squad at Public Safety. Read more at our Campus Police Biography web page.
