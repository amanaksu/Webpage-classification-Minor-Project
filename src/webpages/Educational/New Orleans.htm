About SUNO

Southern University at New Orleans was founded in 1956 to expand academic opportunities for African-Americans. Today, SUNO still serves as a beacon for those looking for educational advancement in an environment that provides personal attention some students need for success. Our mission is to be one of America’s premier urban institutions of higher learning and to graduate students ready to contribute to our city and nation.
