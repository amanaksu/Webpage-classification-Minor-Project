Opportunities and Knowledge

Powerful Outcomes

SHU offers 50+ associate, bachelor’s, master’s and doctoral programs.  

Our innovative programs teach the skills employers value. As a result, 96% of our graduates secure employment or are accepted into graduate programs.

Confidence. Trust. Leadership.

Transformative Experiences

Sacred Heart students begin as learners and grow into leaders.  

In the classroom, in the corporate world and in their personal development, we provide opportunities for our students to accept new responsibilities and challenge them to navigate their lives and careers with confidence and clarity.   

Courageous Leadership

Passionate Service

Sacred Heart students become better innovators, thinkers and leaders through serving others.  

 

Giving back to our community and our world is a fundamental element of the Sacred Heart experience on campus and beyond.

Forward with Bold Vision

Pioneer Spirit

The spirit that built Sacred Heart drives a pioneering, creative, global vision. 

With international campuses, unprecedented growth of our teaching and research facilities and nationally recognized academic programs, we honor our original Pioneer Spirit by creating students who forge their own personal and professional success.
