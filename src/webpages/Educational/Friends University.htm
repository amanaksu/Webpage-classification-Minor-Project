Are you looking for a convenient location to begin college, finish a degree or earn your master's? Do you want the flexibility of choosing on-site or online classes? If so, Friends University offers classes and degree programs across Kansas and online. A centralized Wichita campus serves traditional daytime students and adult bachelor's and master's students. Sites in Lenexa, serving the Greater Kansas City Area, and Topeka have evening bachelor's and master's programs. Online programs allow students to earn a degree or master’s from home.
