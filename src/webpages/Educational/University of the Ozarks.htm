Experience the Ozarks

At Ozarks, we celebrate our location in Clarksville, Arkansas. Nestled in the Ozark Mountains, at the foot of a national forest, and surrounded by scenic waterways, we are reminded of the beauty of nature and our responsibility to be good stewards of the Earth. Come explore "Our Big Back Yard!"

U of O student expands her world through Academic Enrichment Fund

Andrea Dankert's first trip to Argentina five years ago was a living lesson in culture shock, but when she returned over Christmas to see old friends and familiar places, she came away with a new understanding of both herself and this culture that had taken her in as one of their own.

Dankert said her recent reunion was amazing. "This trip wasn't about going as a tourist. It was about becoming a part of another culture."

University Theatre: In the Next Room or The Vibrator Play

October 10, 11, and 12

Nominated for three 2010 Tony Awards, including Best Play; Pulitzer Prize finalist, and winner of the 2010 Will Glickman Award for Best New Play, In the Next Room or The Vibrator Play is a comedy about marriage, intimacy, and electricity.

The Box Office opens at 6:30 p.m. on Thursday and Friday, and at 2:00 p.m. for Saturday's matinee showing. General Admission Tickets are $8.

Buy Tickets Online

Family Weekend Roundup

October 11-13

Join us as we hold an Ozarks Family Weekend Roundup on October 11, 12, and 13!

Family Weekend is always one of the highlights of our year, and this year is no exception. We've planned a weekend of fun, food, entertainment, relaxation, and above all, family and friends!

We hope you'll make plans to be here to share in this celebration because you are an important part of Ozarks!

Register Online

WAIS Presents: Dr. Thomas "Tom" Jones, Habitat for Humanity ambassador-at-large

October 22

Dr. Thomas "Tom" Jones, Habitat for Humanity ambassador-at-large, will speak at Ozarks on Tuesday, October 22.

Jones offers insight into Habitat's history as well as its interfaith and advocacy initiatives. His presentation will take place at 7 p.m. in the Rogers Conference Center, and is free and open to the public.

2013 High School Business Conference

October 23

On Wednesday, October 23, test your business skills at the University of the Ozarks FBLA-PBL Business Conference!

This annual business conference gives high school students an opportunity to compete in a number of different business-oriented events.

For more information, contact Phyllis Johnson by telephone at 479-979-1488 or by email to pjohnson@ozarks.edu.

Register Online

ACT National Exam

October 26

University of the Ozarks will offer a national ACT exam on Saturday, October 26 in Walker Hall. The cost for the exam is $36.50 without the writing portion or $52.50 with the writing portion. The deadline to register is September 27. Registrations after the deadline (through October 11) will incur a $22 late fee.

Ozarks' ACT School Code is: 0120.

Register Online

Arkansas Kennedy Center American College Theatre Festival

October 21-26

University of the Ozarks will host the Arkansas Kennedy Center American College Theatre Festival (KCACTF).KCACTF encourages, recognizes and celebrates the finest and most diverse work produced in university and college theatre programs across the country.

Single show general Admission: $8
Festival pass (all four shows, plus all workshops): $10.
