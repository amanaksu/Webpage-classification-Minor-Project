Explore Nursing Event

The college's Explore Nursing event will be held Saturday, November 9, 9am - noon. The event allows participants to learn about the curriculum, meet instructors, and hear available financial aid options. Participants are encouraged to visit the college's two skills labs and classrooms. A student panel allows participants to directly ask current students questions. Click here to pre-register and view schedule of events.

Parish Nurse Course Offered

Parish nursing is a specialized area of professional nursing practice focusing on the promotion of health and wellness within the context of the values, beliefs and practices of a faith community.  Three college  credits will be granted after successful completion of the course. Click here for more information and registration form.
