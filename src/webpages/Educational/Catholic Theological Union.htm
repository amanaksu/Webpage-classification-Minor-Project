Back-to-School Appeal from President Mark Francis

Please consider making a gift to CTU

"As we start our 46th academic year, please partner with our dedicated faculty and staff and be that helping hand of God to our students. Your gift will be multiplied many times over in the good works of our students." Read Full Appeal.

If you would like to make a gift, please access our secure, online giving form. All gifts to CTU are important and have a tremendous impact on the academic and spiritual life of the school, regardless of their size.

Online Open House

Tuesday, Oct. 22nd, 7 p.m. - 8:30 p.m.

Want to know more about CTU? Prospective students are invited to join us for an online (webinar style) open house on Tuesday, October 22, 2013 from 7:30-8:30pm CST. More information.

Check out CTU's Scholarships and Financial Aid

Apply today!

CTU is committed to working with qualified students to access need and merit-based financial aid in order to make theological and ministerial education accessible.  CTU has a variety of resources from which it draws for financial aid--scholarship funds, matching grants, student employment, and loans.  Learn more.
