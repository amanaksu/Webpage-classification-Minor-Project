Welcome to Northern Michigan University

With over 180 degree programs, a high-tech, high-touch campus and one of America's most liveable communities, NMU is a great place to begin your next adventure.

What's Happening at NMU

No Classes Friday
NMU classes will not be held Friday, Oct. 11 due to a U.P.-wide education planning day. The university will still remain open for business. Classes resume Monday, Oct. 14.
Brass Band Performs  
The Northern Nights series features the St. Louis Brass Band at 7:30 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 12 in Marquette's Kaufman Auditorium. The band has been described as "a wonderful ensemble, both in the technical sense and as an atmosphere that emanates from the stage." Ticket information | watch video
Month of Majors 
October is NMU’s “month of majors” celebration. Each academic department will hold an information session during the month (most in 3301 Hedgcock) to discuss its majors and minors with students. Full schedule | watch video
