Be a power for change.

Antioch University New England offers a transformative education to every student who yearns to make a difference in the world. We don’t take that trust lightly. Our rigorous, flexible, socially relevant, practice-oriented graduate programs help our students and alumni bring about lasting change. Through our learner-centered approach, students achieve the knowledge and skills they need to lead meaningful lives and to advance social, economic, and environmental justice. ...Antioch University New England, because the world needs you now. Read More
