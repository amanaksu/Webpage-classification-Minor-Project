Centennial Celebration Information

October 3-5, 2013

Complete this form to register for the event.

We have arranged for housing during the Centennial with two properties.

The Hampton Inn & Suites North, 2870 59th St. NW, Rochester (a 2-minute drive from the Rochester Assembly of God where the sessions will be held on Friday and Saturday, October 4 and 5). Rates are $95 per night plus tax. Amenities include: Complimentary  hot breakfast, parking, wi-fi and wired internet service.

You may phone for reservations at 507-289-6100 or you may reserve your room by going to this link: http://hamptoninn.hilton.com/en/hp/groups/personalized/R/RSTNOHX-CRC-20131003/index.jhtml?WT.mc_id=POG


The Kahler Inn & Suites,  20 SW Second Avenue, Rochester (a few minute drive from the Rochester Assembly of God). Rates are $89 per night plus tax. Amenities include: Complimentary parking & continental breakfast, wi-fi, microwave, and refrigerator. You may phone for reservations at 507-280-6200 or you may reserve your room by going to this link: http://kahler.com/group/crossroads-college.php

 

If you call for reservations remember to tell the reservation clerk that you are with the Crossroads College Centennial Celebration.
