Accessible Christian Education

Nazarene Bible College is accessible to students around the world online or on our beautiful Colorado Springs campus.

Financial Aid Available

You may be eligible for Pell Grant, federal student loans, merit scholarships, and other scholarships to help pay for college expenses.

Nationally and Regionally Accredited

Nazarene Bible College is accredited both nationally and regionally meeting the same standards as state universities and local community colleges.

Transferable Credits

Thanks to our strong accreditation, credits should transfer easily to a wide variety of other colleges and universities.

Christian Ministry

Nazarene Bible College is an undergraduate professional school of Christian ministry, committed to academic and practical programs designed to educate students for service and leadership in a diverse world.
