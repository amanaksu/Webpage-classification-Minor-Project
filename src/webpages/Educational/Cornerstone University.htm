Traditional Undergrad

The undergraduate programs at CU educate students intellectually, spiritually, and professionally.

Students think critically, develop their creative gifts, grow spiritually, and expand their cross-cultural capabilities. The result is a college degree that not only prepares students for success in today’s world, but also empowers them to engage the cultures of our world for Christ and His Kingdom.

Faculty

Cornerstone University professors bring expertise to the classroom.

Our faculty members frequently participate in professional development activities, ranging from attending and speaking at conferences around the U.S. and in many other countries to authoring books and other academic publications. 

Organizations

CU offers many organizations  and opportunities to help better prepare students for their future.

Our Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE) chapter has been nationally recognized, and the Institute for Christianity and Cultural Engagement (ICCE) hosts conferences and guest lecturers who explore different aspects of what it means to be a Christian in today’s society.

Build A Life That Matters

We aspire to create a thriving community of fully devoted followers of Jesus in a spiritually contagious, academically excellent, richly resourced, and culturally diverse learning environment that attracts outstanding students, faculty, and staff who influence our world for Christ, his church, and his kingdom.

Spiritual Life on Campus

Cornerstone University's campus culture is genuine, welcoming, and sincere. CU's unique spiritual atmosphere is shaped by the students, who desire to pursue Christ in their everyday activities. Come visit! Experience a day in this community where Jesus Christ is central.

Adult & Graduate Studies

The Professional & Graduate Studies Division

exists to empower the adult learner to influence the world by providing a distinctive and academically excellent education from a Christ-centered worldview. Accelerated degrees, online classes, and convenient campus locations make earning your degree not only possible, but manageable too!

Seminary

Grand Rapids Theological Seminary

is a graduate school that prepares biblically and culturally informed ministry leaders for service through Christ’s church. With high standards of academic excellence, GRTS equips ministry leaders for global service to current and future generations.

CU Online

Cornerstone University offers online studies

in order to deliver high-quality education programs to working professionals who find it difficult or impossible to attend class in a physical location. This is a university-wide initiative providing coordination and support of the three educational divisions of Cornerstone University in the area of online education, all instructed from a Christ-centered worldview.

Global Opportunities

The PGS Global Studies Program

 

Cornerstone University believes you can make a difference in the global community and has created the Global Studies Program to develop you into a knowledgeable and effective leader. Through short international journeys, students interact with kingdom companies and social enterprise, develop their cultural intelligence and experience societies being transformed.

For Parents

College is an investment, both for students and parents. Your new student may be filled with questions about life at CU, or your current student might need some help navigating college life - but we understand there are also some questions that parents would like answered. As such, we have devoted this section to you, the parents.

Schedule a Visit

Whether you’re from the area or from out of state (or even out of country), there’s nothing like a first-hand experience at Cornerstone University. From a tour of campus to time with admissions counselors who can answer your questions in person, a visit to campus is the best way to learn more about and get the “feel” of CU.

Getting Started

As a parent of a new college student, things can get pretty overwhelming - there's so much to do and remember! That's why we've set up a one-stop-shop for everything a CU parent needs to know. From questions, to hotel recommendations, to health services info, we've got you covered.

Alumni Newsboard

Keep up-to-date on alumni events, news, and more through our alumni newsboard, a one-stop shop for all things alumni. 

Alumni & Friends 5K

When you participate in the 19th Annual Alumni & Friends 5K you are supporting Cornerstone University student athletes. The race is a flat, fast 5K around the campus, starting at the Hansen Athletic Center and finishing in De Witt baseball stadium at home plate.

Alumni Awards

The annual distinguished alumni awards of Cornerstone University and Grand Rapids Theological Seminary recognize outstanding alumni who have achieved success in their personal and professional lives or who have distinguished themselves through volunteer service to the university. You are invited to nominate alumni who fit the criteria of the six alumni awards.
