Fall Festival and College Preview Day

Interested in attending Oak Hills?  Check us out on Oct. 17 & 18.  Register for College Preview Day, Oct. 18, for your chance to tour campus, meet faculty & staff, and sit in on a class.  Come early on Oct. 17 for Fall Festival and help judge our chili cook-off, go for a hayride, and stay in the dorms with a current student!  Reserve your spot today by registering online or calling 866-307-OHCC.

Camp Oak Hills Prayer Request
