YEARS OF SERVICE: Sister Grace Mary Flickinger, S.B.S. (left), biology professor and faculty athletic representative, and Dorothy Sterlings (right), Office of Admissions records analyst, are congratulated by XU President Norman Francis for 45 and 40 years of service at a special Founder's Day reception. A total of 74 long-time faculty and staff members were honored.Marlene Robinson, director of Graduate Admissions, was also recognized for 40 years.

WELCOME HOME: U.S. Magistrate Judge Karen Wells Roby '83 visits with seniors (L-R) Michael Seaberry, Shelby Haynes, and Gianni Bahamduring Xavier's annual Founder's Day Convocation, at which she delivered the keynote address.

THE REAL DEAL: Highly-successful American rapper, author, and producer Chuck D (center), arguably best known as the leader of the socially conscious mid-1980s rap group Public Enemy, gets personal with XU students Lucas Johnson and Paige Jones following his well-received campus lecture on Race, Rap, and Reality.

THE HEIGHT OF FASHION: Twenty-five business majors received Division of Business jackets and collectively pledged to uphold the Division's Business Code of Conduct during the 2nd annual Blazer Ceremony.Enjoying the moment are: (L-R) Michael Arnelle, Kaila Pulliam-Collins, XU Business Chair Dr. Joe Ricks, guest speaker Whitney Brown '08, and Brittany Nicholas.

GRAND TOUR: Louisiana Attorney General James "Buddy" Caldwell (center) visits the St. Katharine Drexel Chapel during a campus tour, accompanied by (L-R) Director of Minority Affairs and Government Liaison (Litigation Division) Joe Fuller, Deputy Director V. Elaine Patin, COP Dean Dr. Kathleen Kennedy, President Dr. Norman C. Francis., and Provost/Senior VP for Academic Affairs Dr. Loren Blanchard.

GLOBEMED: A new organization seeking to empower students and communities to work together to improve the health of people living in poverty around the world has formed at XU. Current leadership includes (L-R) CIIP Director Torian Lee, chapter co-presidents Jamilla Emerson and Raven Holmes (the founding member), and faculty advisor Dr. Krista Mincey. Contact xula@globemed.org for more info.

IT'S A SMALL WORLD: Nine international students from France, Pakistan, and Brazil are studying at Xavier this year before heading back to their home universities abroad. The group includes (front row L-R) Nayara Lopes, Gabriela Barbarini, Lorena Fernandes, Melissa Belet, and Sara Rodrigues; (back row)Hidayatullah Jamote, Claudio Duarte, Murilo Perez, and Raphael Pinheiro. Additional students will arrive in January.

MAKING IT REAL: Xavier business majors (in front of table, L-R) Brittany Nicholas, Justin Bowers, and Hue Hilliary Nguyen check out career opportunities with representatives of the Brennan's Restaurant Group during the Fall Career Expo at the Mercedes-Benz Superdome.
