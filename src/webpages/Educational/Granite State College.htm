Welcome to Granite State College

Granite State College has been empowering our students to achieve their dreams for almost 40 years. We are dedicated to providing you with an unparalleled, quality education with the flexibility to learn when you want, where you want and how you want. With the ability to take classes either on campus or online, our class schedules adapt to your busy lifestyle. Our diverse associate, bachelor's and master's degrees, in addition to our post-baccalaureate teacher certification program, offer you more choices to succeed in your desired career path. Small class sizes, affordable tuition and convenient locations all over the state, make Granite State College New Hampshire's #1 college for adults of all ages.

GSC’s online bachelor’s programs are among the nation’s best, according to the U.S. News and World Report rankings.



We're committed to providing an excellent education for an excellent value, and we're proud to have one of the most affordable tuition rates in our region.

 

Our dedication to helping students improve their lives has earned us a ranking by Washington Monthly as the #5 liberal arts college in the U.S. for Social Mobility.
