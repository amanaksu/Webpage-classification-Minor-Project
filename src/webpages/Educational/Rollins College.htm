Undergraduate Programs

Connect to the intellectual, social, and creative talents that will make you a success in life. Classes are small and you’ll work directly with your professors. Rollins’ world-class, hands-on liberal arts education offers you a wide range of majors and minors in our full-time and evening undergraduate programs.


Graduate Programs

Rollins’ top rated MBA, Executive Doctorate in Business Administration (EDBA), and evening graduate programs connect you with the concepts, skills, and professional networks that best help you realize your aspirations.


Continuing Education

For nondegree seekers, Rollins offers several training and education options. These include the Philanthropy & Nonprofit Leadership Center, the Rollins Center for Lifelong Learning, and the Rollins Management and Executive Education Center.


Winter Park Institute

The Winter Park Institute features discussions between the Rollins community and thought leaders from diverse fields.

The Alfond Inn

Enjoy the boutique atmosphere with amenities to rival even the most luxurious hotels at The Alfond Inn at Rollins College.
