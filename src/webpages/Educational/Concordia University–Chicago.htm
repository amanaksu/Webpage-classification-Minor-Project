The Latest

  • CUC's annual reading conference features local author and reading expert

    The Concordia University Chicago's 38th annual reading conference takes place October 19. This year’s highlights include author and local reading expert Anne Goudvis, EdD, and a special lunch with Becky Noble, librarian at St. Luke’s Elementary School. Registration for this event is now open.

  • Lectures in Church Music plans two-year celebration

    The annual Lectures in Chuch Music series begins a two-year celebration of CUC's 150th anniversary on Sunday, October 20 through Tuesday, October 22.  Lectures and speakers' series will take place on the CUC campus.  All musical performances will be held at Grace Lutheran Church in River Forest.

  • Concordia University Chicago presents first-ever Ethics Leadership Summit

    Concordia University Chicago will host its debut Ethics Leadership Summit on Friday, October 18, from 9:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Space is limited and early registration is advised.
