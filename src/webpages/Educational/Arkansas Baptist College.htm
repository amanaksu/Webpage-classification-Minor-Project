Mission Statement:

Arkansas Baptist College prepares students for a life of service grounded in academic scholarship, the liberal arts tradition, social responsibility, Christian development and preparation for employment in a global community.
Arkansas Baptist College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools located at 30 North LaSalle Street, Suite 2400, Chicago, IL. 60602-2504. Higher Learning Commission Website: www.ncahigherlearningcommission.org.
