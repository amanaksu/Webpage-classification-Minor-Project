Roland Park - News & Events
Founded in 1791, St. Mary's Seminary & University in Baltimore is the first Catholic seminary established in the
United States. For over two hundred years, St. Mary’s has been owned and operated by the Sulpician Fathers,
a community of diocesan priests dedicated to the formation of priests.
