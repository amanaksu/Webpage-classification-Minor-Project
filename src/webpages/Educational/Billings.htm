  • The College of Allied Health Professions is our newest college. With thriving Bachelor and Master degree programs it's also one of the fastest growing. Being located in Montana's healthcare hub also provides students a great opportunity to put their acquired skills to the test. Learn more.

  • The College of Arts and Sciences boasts diverse liberal arts curriculum and rich student /faculty engagement. Outstanding teachers and researchers connect with students in labs and classrooms to provide the foundation for the next steps in graduate school, professional school or careers. Learn more.
  • The College of Business is in an elite class in delivering business education. The AACSB seal of excellence coupled with dynamic faculty-student interactions has created a learning environment that prepares students for success in areas of marketing, management, accounting or finance. Learn more.
  • The College of Education is Montana's premier school for educators. Students have the best of both worlds when it comes to hands-on, practical learning. Theory can be put into action with experience in state's largest school district or some of region's finest rural schools. Learn more.
  • City College at MSU Billings is at the forefront of community education, training and workforce development. MSU Billings' two-year college is nimble enough to meet the technical needs of the region while providing Associate degrees that offer a seamless transfer to the four-year campus. Learn more.
