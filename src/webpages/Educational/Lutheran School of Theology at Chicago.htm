LSTC faculty, students, and alumni part of Reformation conference in Sweden

Barbara Rossing, Vitor Westhelle, and Richard Perry will make presentations at “Remembering the Past – Living the Future: Lutheran Tradition in Transition” in Uppsala Sweden Oct. 8-10. Students Jonathan Wilson and Carl-Eric Gentes are also presenting papers, along with five LSTC alumni, including Monica  Jyotsna Melanchthon.
