About Bowie State

Welcome

Located in the heart of the Washington-Baltimore-Annapolis region, Bowie State boasts a best-of-all-worlds learning experience—high-quality undergraduate and graduate programs delivered in a convenient and welcoming environment at affordable tuition rates.

This unique combination empowers you to gain the advanced knowledge and leadership skills you need to excel in today's competitive climate. Your goals and our passion—a potent combination!

Sustainability

Here at Bowie State University, we recognize our responsibility to take care of the earth for ourselves and future generations. We fully understand the importance of sustaining the natural resources that we all share. As an institution of higher learning, we also know that we must play a vital role in educating the environmental stewards of today for a better tomorrow.
