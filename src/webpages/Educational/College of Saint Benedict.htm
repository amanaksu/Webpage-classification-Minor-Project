Annual Peace Studies Conference focuses on Civil Rights Movement

The 26th Annual Peace Studies Conference will be held from 1 to 9 p.m. Monday, Oct. 14, in the Quadrangle Building and Stephen B. Humphrey Theater, SJU. The theme is "Acting on Faith: Nonviolence and the Struggle for Interracial Justice, Then and Now."

Writers from faculty, community to read at CSB

The Literary Arts Institute at CSB welcomes seven writers for a reading at 7 p.m. Wednesday, Oct. 16 in the Teresa Reception Center boardroom, Main Building, CSB.

BVC gets men involved in helping others around the world

Now in its 11th year, approximately 125 SJU graduates have served in the Benedictine Volunteer Crops (BVC), including 17 men at nine sites during 2013-14.

Author Annette Atkins to sign books at CSB Oct. 12

CSB will be hosting a book signing by author Annette Atkins on Saturday, Oct. 12, as part of Family Weekend festivities.

Concert to feature world premiere of song written for CSB Centennial

Pastiche, the faculty chamber music ensemble of CSB and SJU, presents its fall concert at 7:30 p.m. Friday, Oct. 11, at Escher Auditorium, Benedicta Arts Center of the College of Saint Benedict.

Now accepting 2014 Reunion Award nominations

The CSB Alumnae Relations Office is accepting 2014 Reunion Award nominations now through Monday, Oct. 24. Please help us honor your friends, your classmates, the extraordinary women of Saint Ben's - by nominating someone today.

Saint John's announces $160 million capital campaign

On Friday, Sept. 27, 2013 Saint John's Abbey and University announced their latest and most ambitious capital campaign, Forward Ever Forward. The goal for the campaign, which runs through 2016, is $160 million, of which $108 million has already been committed in gifts, pledges and future expectancies.

Rachel sisters enjoy special day

September 21st marked a big day for CSB senior Chelsea Rachel. For the first time in her life, the outside hitter lined up on the opposite side of her sister, Shelby Rachel, who is a first-year outside hitter for St. Catherine University.

Draxler earns medalist honors as MIAC golf title returns to Collegeville

SJU ended its fall season with the program's 11th conference title at the 2013 MIAC Championship. Junior Brett Draxler became the 10th Johnnie in school history to earn medalist honors.
