  • About PBA

    Enlightening Minds ... Enriching Souls ... Extending Hands ...

    Founded in 1968, Palm Beach Atlantic is an interdenominational Christian university with some 50 undergraduate majors (including nursing). Graduate degrees in business, leadership, psychology and pharmacy are offered. Located in the Palm Beaches of southeast Florida, PBA is an ideal place to grow and explore your faith with young people of many races, nationalities and creeds.
     
     

  • In PBA News

    Former Attorney General to Speak at Commencement

    Former U.S. Attorney General and U.S. Senator John Ashcroft will be the featured speaker at PBA's Fall Commencement Dec. 14. "General Ashcroft has distinguished himself through his unwavering commitment to his faith, family and country,” said PBA President William M. B. Fleming Jr. "Palm Beach Atlantic is proud to host such a dedicated public servant."
     

  • Focus On

    University Advances Six Spots in Latest U.S. News College Rankings Guide

    PBA is ranked 44th among the best universities in the South according to U.S. News & World Report’s Best Colleges 2014 edition released September 9. The publication includes rankings of more than 1,400 schools nationwide. U.S. News gathers data from each college on up to 16 indicators of academic excellence.
     

  • AFE

    Outback Steakhouse Founder Gannon to be Honored by PBA

    The University, in partnership with Bank of America, will present the 2013 American Free Enterprise Medal to Tim Gannon, a co-founder of Outback Steakhouse, Inc. and local resident. Gannon will be recognized along with companion medalists Paul Donahue, president and CEO of G4S Government Solutions, Inc., and Kenneth Kennerly, executive director of The Honda Classic during a Nov. 7 ceremony.

    Dig into the 32-page 2012-2013 President's Report to gain a deeper understanding of where Palm Beach Atlantic University is today and where we are headed in the future.   • Athletics

    PBA's Sailfish at Home in Top-Notch Greene Complex

    Many of Palm Beach Atlantic's teams play in the outstanding Greene Complex for Sports and Recreation -- a 65,000 square foot facility located in the heart of campus that houses the Rubin Arena, locker rooms, athletic training facility, fitness center, recreational space, PBA Sports Hall of Fame and the PBA Athletic Department.
