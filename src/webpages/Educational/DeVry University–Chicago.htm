This website has been created specifically for our students, faculty and staff members. Here you can find information about the broad array of student services at DeVry Chicago including part-time job opportunities and library assistance information. Explore this site to learn more about services offered at our Chicago campus as well as our Centers in Chicago Loop, Chicago O'Hare and Gurnee. Prepare for your future employment by getting involved in student activities and networking opportunities.
