Welcome to Rosalind Franklin University 
At Rosalind Franklin University, we offer our students exceptional clinical education, research opportunities and exposure to emerging technologies. From day one, students collaborate in healthcare teams, learning from, with, and about one another's profession, and how, together, they each impact the future of medicine.
