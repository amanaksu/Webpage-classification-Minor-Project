Henderson Homecoming 2013 will be Saturday, October 12. Events for alumni, friends and students are planned Monday, Oct. 7 through Sunday, Oct. 13. The Reddies will be playing Southwestern Oklahoma State, with a special recognition of the 1963 Championship Football Team. See More...

The investiture of Dr. Glen Jones as 17th president of Henderson State University will be held Oct. 29 at 2 p.m. in Arkansas Hall. A reception will follow on the South Lawn.  See More...

Jennifer Boyett of Conway will join Henderson State University as the new Vice President of University Advancement beginning Oct. 15.   See More...

Henderson's new full-service Starbucks in Garrison Center is open! Other campus improvements include landscaping projects on the South Lawn and other areas, upgrades in the residence halls and renovations to the Garrison Day Gym. See More...

Henderson State University will offer new bachelor’s degrees in criminal justice and engineering physics this fall, along with a new online master’s degree in education technology leadership. New degree tracks in statistics (mathematics) and biochemistry will also be available. See More...

Henderson's planetarium in Reynolds Science Center has scheduled three shows open to the public this fall. All shows begin at 7 p.m. Admission is $1 for students with a university ID and $3 for others. See More...
