The Lord of the Rings and Other Books by and about J.R.R. Tolkien



J.R.R. Tolkien (1892-1973) was a devout Roman Catholic, professor of Middle English at Oxford University, friend of C.S. Lewis, and popular author. He is best known for his children's story The Hobbit and the epic fantasy The Lord of the Rings.
