Vishnu Statues

Vishnu is one of the principal gods of Hinduism. Vishnu's vehicle is the swift-flying bird Garuda that can spread the Vedic knowledge with great courage. He rests on the bed of the powerful, coiled serpent with 1000 head, Seshanag who represents the sleeping universe. Vishnu is the Lord of protection and maintenance. His consort Lakshmi is the goddess of wealth. Learn more in our article on Vishnu.
