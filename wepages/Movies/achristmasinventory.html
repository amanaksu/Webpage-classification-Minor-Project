A CHRISTMAS INVENTORY (INVENTARIO DE NATAL) (director/writer: Miguel Gomes; screenwriter: ; cinematographer: Rui Pocas; editor:  Manuel Mozos; cast: Alberto Seixas Santos, Maria Joana Gracio, Paulo Encarnacio, Manuel Mozos; Runtime: 23; MPAA Rating: NR; producers: Sandro Aguilar/Joao Figueriras; New Wave Films; 2012-Portugal-in Portuguese with English subtitles)

 
"It playfully blends together fiction and reality."

Reviewed by Dennis Schwartz

Portuguese filmmaker Miquel Gomes ("The Face You Deserve"/"The Beloved Month of August"/"Tabu") began as a film critic and then moved into making oddball image rich short films. A Christmas Inventory is a tale of a remembered family Christmas gathering together. It's shot as  “a fake documentary and a fake animation film, a semi-fiction about children who go to war, play music, and take over.” It playfully blends together fiction and reality.

If you want to see something different, this pic is different.

REVIEWED ON 6/11/2013       GRADE: B-

Dennis Schwartz: "Ozus' World Movie Reviews"
